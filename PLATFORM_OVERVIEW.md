In simple terms: **TraceVault** is a secure "Digital Locker" and "Investigation Engine." It ensures that once you find a piece of digital evidence, it is protected, analyzed, and ready for the courtroom.

Let's explore the **Complete Feature Set** through the lens of a real-life investigation.

## 🎬 Case Study: "The Mumbai Bank Cyber Fraud"

A major bank in Mumbai discovers that ₹50 Crore has been illegally transferred out of their accounts. The bank's board of directors is under pressure—they need solid proof to take to the Cyber Cell and the Courts.

Here is how TraceVault’s features are used to solve this massive investigation:

### 1. Unified Case Management (The Digital Command Center)
*   **Usage in this Case:** The Lead Investigator, **Priya**, opens a new case: *"Mumbai Bank Fraud."* Instead of losing evidence in scattered emails, the entire team (Forensics, Legal, and IT) works in this one secure "War Room." Jennifer can read all briefings and see the status of every lead in real-time.

### 2. Evidence Hardening & Artifact Registry (The Digital Wax Seal)
*   **Usage in this Case:** Priya finds a suspicious Excel sheet of bank transactions. TraceVault immediately captures its unique "Digital Fingerprint" (SHA-256). If the hacker tries to edit the file later to remove their name, TraceVault shows a "Tampered" alert, proving the file was altered.

### 3. Immutable Chain of Custody (The Digital Logbook)
*   **Usage in this Case:** Priya sends the transactions to **Inspector Kumar**. TraceVault records the Exact Time, the Inspector’s name, and his computer’s IP address. In court, this log proves that the evidence was handled only by authorized people and was never "planted" or changed.

### 4. Advanced Forensic Analysis (The Fake File Detector)
*   **Usage in this Case:** A file looks like a random photo (`Office_Group.jpg`), but TraceVault’s **Deep Logic** engine notices the internal chaos is too high. It reveals the "photo" is actually a hidden, encrypted folder of stolen bank data. It detects the "lie" using **Shannon Entropy** and **Magic Byte** validation.

### 5. Threat Intelligence & Anomaly Detection (The Early Warning System)
*   **Usage in this Case:** TraceVault automatically alerts Priya about an **Anomaly**: A user logged in from a strange city at 3:00 AM to view the transaction logs. This "Threat Intelligence" helps Priya identify the suspect (an internal employee) before they can delete the logs.

### 6. Interactive Relationship Graph (The Suspect Mapper)
*   **Usage in this Case:** Navigating to the **Intelligence Graph**, Priya sees a visual link between the "Stolen Excel Sheet" and three different user accounts. This graph visualization makes it easy to see how the data moved between the suspect and the external hackers.

### 7. Real-Time Forensic Timeline (The Crime Clock)
*   **Usage in this Case:** Priya opens the **Activity Timeline**. It shows a second-by-second "Clock" of events—exactly when the file was created, when it was encrypted, and when it was first leaked. This timeline is the heartbeat of the investigation.

### 8. Ledger Anchoring & Public Verification (The Global Proof)
*   **Usage in this Case:** To ensure 100% trust, TraceVault "Anchors" the case hash to a public ledger. Even if someone hacks the entire bank server, they cannot change the anchor on the ledger. This provides a "Timestamp of Truth" that no one can argue with.

### 9. Role-Based Access Control (The Need-to-Know Guard)
*   **Usage in this Case:** Priya uses **User Management** to ensure the Bank Manager can see the "Summary" but cannot see the "Technical Recovery Keys." This protects the investigation from internal interference and accidental deletions.

### 10. Intelligent Global Search (The Needle-in-a-Haystack Finder)
*   **Usage in this Case:** Priya needs to find one specific "Transaction ID" across 5,000 files. She uses the **Global Search** feature, which scans metadata, filenames, and logs instantly, finding the "Smoking Gun" document in seconds.

### 11. Professional Forensic Reporting (The Courtroom Dossier)
*   **Usage in this Case:** Finally, Priya clicks "Generate PDF Report." She gets a professional, watermarked document (ISO 27037 compliant) containing every hash, every log, and every forensic finding. This report is what Jennifer presents to the Judge to win the case.
